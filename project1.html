Low Poly Charactor

Overview

I will make a 3D male charactor. I will animate him to stride along and also to wave

My end users will be male teenagers between 13 and 15 who want a male character that they can aspire to be and look up to when playing the game. They would like a charactor that looks like the person they want to be so i'll make him tall and strong and buff with wide shoulders.

  • Arms
